CUSTOM WEB DESIGN & HANDS ON DEVELOPMENT.

About Us

Amlaw Design is a full service web development team that designs, builds, deploys, and hosts websites for small to mid sized businesses in the Boston area. We deliver websites suitable for e-commerce (with full credit card integration) that are custum built to suit our clients needs. Our websites incorporate custom content management systems (cms) so that business owners can update and modify their websites themselves without any need to understand the technologies used to create them.

Modern technology.

Technologies Used

Amlaw Design uses the latest technologies and stays up to date to keep our websites optimized for user experience, functionality, and search engine optimization (seo). Depending on specific needs we develop using industry standard languages and frameworks such as PHP, Python, Django, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Javascript, Bootstrap, Tailwind, CSS, and HTML. Our sites are prepared to be deployed on any server that implements the latest technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes. All of these technologies allow us to build up to date websites with full functionality and security.
