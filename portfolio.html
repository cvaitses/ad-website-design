Audioproz

Audioproz has a custom content management system which allows them to easily list new items as well as place ads and educational material. Audioproz is a responsive site using Bootstap and custom css. An e-commerce platform was developed for Audioproz including a shopping cart and paypal/cc checkout system, but ultimately they preferred to handle requests by email due to the uniqueness of most of the items listed.
